    Learning (AI/ML) for staying ahead of the curve. What is important for

    Blockchain, is a technology that uses a sequential set of transactions

    across stacks for easy implementations. Having reusable and expandable blocks for

    will be a topic for 2021 to enhance security methods for specialized data
    services based on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to differentiate
    from software development norms.

    The way to stay ahead is to know the list of technologies that touch a domain and
    achieve proficiency in them for incorporation into tasks to maintain productivity.
    The five themes for 2021 for maintaining relevance in AI/ML: Blockchain,
    Cloud, Ethical Artificial Intelligence (AI), Interoperability,

  2. Telehealth and telemedicine
    3.

    by Anis Uzzaman

    Source: https://www.inc.com/anis-uzzaman/top-business-technology-trends-in-2021
    Institutions, especially in health care, are working to lower the exposure of Covid-19
    to patients and workers. Many private and public practices have started implementing more
    telehealth offerings such as doctor-patient video chats, A.I. avatar-based diagnostics,
    and no-contact-based medication delivery.

    Telehealth visits have surged by 50 percent compared with pre-pandemic levels. IHS Technology predicted that 70 million Americans would use telehealth by 2020. Since then, Forrester Research predicted the number of U.S. virtual care visits will reach almost a billion early in 2021.

    Teladoc Health, Amwell, Livongo Health, One Medical, and Humana are some of the public
    companies offering telehealth services to meet the current needs. Startups are not far behind. Startups like MDLive, MeMD, iCliniq, K Health, 98point6, Sense.ly, and Eden Health have also contributed toward meeting the growing needs in 2020, and will continue offering creative solutions in 2021. Beyond telehealth, in 2021 we can expect to see health care advancements in biotech and A.I., as well as machine learning opportunities (example: Suki AI) to support diagnosis, admin work, and robotic health care.
